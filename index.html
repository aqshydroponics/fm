What is Hydroponics?

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil, using water enriched with nutrients to deliver everything the plant needs directly to the roots. This sustainable farming technique promotes faster growth, higher yields, and efficient use of resources, making it ideal for urban and large-scale farming.
