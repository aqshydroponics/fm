Hydroponics: The Future of Sustainable Farming

What is Hydroponics?

Hydroponics is a soilless farming method that uses water mixed with essential nutrients to grow plants. It is one of the fastest growing agricultural techniques in the world because it saves space, reduces water consumption, and allows plants to grow faster and healthier compared to traditional farming.
